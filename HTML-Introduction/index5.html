Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Combine warm milk, yeast, and sugar in a large mixing bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add egg, egg yolk, melted butter, and salt and mix until combined.
  3. Add flour and mix until dough comes together. Knead dough for 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl and cover. Let rise for 1 hour.
  5. Roll dough out into a 14x9 inch rectangle. Spread softened butter over dough.
  6. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle over buttered dough.
  7. Roll dough into a tight log. Cut into 12 rolls.
  8. Place rolls in a greased 9x13 inch baking pan. Cover and let rise for 30 minutes.
  9. Bake rolls in a 375 degree oven for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. While rolls are baking, prepare the cream cheese frosting.
  11. Spread frosting over warm rolls before serving.
